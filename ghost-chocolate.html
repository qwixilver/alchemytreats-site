Ghost Chocolate

Ethereal chocolate magic with a whisper of toffee—vanishing sweetness in every bite.

Flavor Profile

Ghost Chocolate is a fleeting kiss of cocoa — a delicate, whisper-light blend reminiscent of white chocolate and soft toffee. It’s smooth, subtly sweet, and barely there… until you find yourself craving just one more.

Texture & Experience

A gentle dissolve on the tongue with no lingering aftertaste. Designed to feel light, soft, and clean— as if the chocolate is passing through another dimension before it reaches your taste buds.

Great For

  • Chocolate lovers who prefer elegance over intensity
  • Those with sensitive taste buds or subtle palates
  • Pairing with warm milk, coffee, or quiet reflection
  • Anyone intrigued by flavor that feels like a memory

Love Potion #1

Enchanting dark chocolate and raspberry swirl, a bewitching spell of rich indulgence.

Flavor Profile

Love Potion #1 captures the decadence of dark chocolate and the bold tang of ripe raspberries. This rich duo blends into a flavor as smooth as a truffle and as punchy as a spell gone right. It’s love at first bite—or maybe obsession.

Texture & Experience

Softer than our typical hard candies, Love Potion #1 offers a semi-chewy texture that melts slowly and leaves a velvety finish. Close your eyes, and it's like biting into a bite-sized brownie soaked in raspberry elixir.
